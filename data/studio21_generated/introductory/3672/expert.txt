 Given a string of integers, return the number of odd-numbered substrings that can be formed.  For example, in the case of `"1341"`, they are `1, 1, 3, 13, 41, 341, 1341`, a total of `7` numbers.  `solve("1341") = 7`.  See test cases for more examples. Good luck!
