 Given a binary string s (a string consisting only of '0' and '1's). Return the number of substrings with all characters 1's. Since the answer may be too large, return it modulo 10^9 + 7.   Example 1: Input: s = "0110111" Output: 9 Explanation: There are 9 substring in total with only 1's characters. "1" -> 5 times. "11" -> 3 times. "111"
